## ‚ùå DON'T SAY

**Avoid Bragging:**
- ‚ùå "11.6 million AIS positions"
- ‚ùå "17,000+ vessels tracked"
- ‚ùå "Most comprehensive AIS database"
- ‚ùå "More data than competitors"
- ‚ùå Any specific large numbers

**Why:** 
- Sounds like showing off
- Might upset data providers
- Creates unrealistic expectations (users start with 0)
- Could violate terms of service

**Avoid Taking Credit:**
- ‚ùå "We provide AIS data"
- ‚ùå "Our AIS coverage"
- ‚ùå "Mari8X's global vessel network"

**Why:**
- AISstream.io provides the data, not us
- Be grateful and respectful
- Give credit where due

**Avoid Unrealistic Promises:**
- ‚ùå "Guaranteed 100% uptime"
- ‚ùå "Never miss a vessel"
- ‚ùå "Real-time updates every second"

**Why:**
- Free tier has limitations
- We can't guarantee what we don't control
- Be honest about constraints
